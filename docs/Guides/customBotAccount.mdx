The custom bot account feature allows you to use your own account instead of the RoManager accounts.

## Getting your bot account cookie

:::tip

We recommend you make a dedicated Roblox account to use as your bot account.

:::

The `bot-account add` command only accepts a cookie, not username and password. Follow these steps to get your cookie (browser-specific):

<details><summary>Chrome</summary>
<p>

1. Open an incognito window.

2. Open https://roblox.com and log in with the bot account you want to use.

3. Once logged in, click the padlock icon next to the URL, and click `Cookies`.

![](https://i.jaydensar.net/2021/08/F3e0kP4y7i8a8y.png)

4. Once this menu opens up, expand `roblox.com` => `Cookies` => `.ROBLOSECURITY`.

![](https://i.jaydensar.net/2021/08/FPk6O0J0MNhNzn.png)

5. Copy the entirety of `Content`.

![](https://i.jaydensar.net/2021/08/xG1NO7Om6xXDAw.png)

</p>
</details>

<details><summary>Firefox</summary>
<p>

1. Open an incognito window.

2. Open https://roblox.com and log in with the bot account you want to use.

3. Once logged in, right click anywhere on the page, then click `Inspect (Q)`. Then click the `Storage` tab.

![](https://i.jaydensar.net/2021/08/W5KcGckDWfBmVr.png)

4. On the sidebar, expand `Cookies` then `https://www.roblox.com`.

![](https://i.jaydensar.net/2021/08/LE67mrFNRKajEJ.png)

5. Double click on the `Value` column next to .ROBLOSECURITY, then copy it.

![](https://i.jaydensar.net/2021/08/eWcpilPKic6gV4.png)

</p>
</details>

## Adding your bot account to RoManager

**Open DMs with RoManager as you cannot run these commands in a server.**

Next, run this command:

<DiscordMessages>
	<DiscordMessage avatar="https://cdn.discordapp.com/embed/avatars/5.png">
		.bot-account add (your cookie here, including the warning part)
	</DiscordMessage>
	<DiscordMessage
		author="RoManager"
		verified={true}
		bot={true}
		avatar="https://cdn.discordapp.com/avatars/738035113815834746/9c943bdaadc550fc4d7af38c6d2bc8ba.png"
	>
		<discord-reply
			slot="reply"
			avatar="https://cdn.discordapp.com/embed/avatars/5.png"
		>
			.bot-account add (your cookie here, including the warning part)
		</discord-reply>
		Successfully added bot account.
	</DiscordMessage>
</DiscordMessages>

## Using your bot account in a server

To link your bot account to a server, join the group you're going to set up if you haven't already, then run the `setup` command with the `--bot-account` flag, with the bot account username:

<DiscordMessages>
	<DiscordMessage avatar="https://cdn.discordapp.com/embed/avatars/5.png">
		.setup (group name or id) --bot-account (bot account username or id)
	</DiscordMessage>
</DiscordMessages>
